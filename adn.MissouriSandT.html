Mobile Apps

The S&T App

Get quick access to news, the Joe'SS student account portal, course catalog, calendars, campus directory, dining, maps and sports with the S&T App. For comments, questions or help with the S&T app, contact mobilesupport@mst.edu.

Missouri S&T
Android App Privacy Policy

Missouri S&T’s Android App (henceforth, The App) does not collect or transfer any data to us or to any third party apart from the circumstances explained below.

Issues Emergency Calls

The App requests access to be able to place emergency calls to 911 and the Campus Police only. It does not record or transfer any phone data to us or any third party.

Access to Joe’ss

The App does require your Missouri S&T username and password to access Joe’SS for such items as grades, schedules and course catalog. In all cases data transferred is to the phone via web services. No data is transmitted to be stored by us or any third party.

Monitor for Calls

The App does monitor phone state to mute the audio stream from KMST during an incoming call. No data is used, stored or transmitted.

Access GPS

The App does display current location on Maps when GPS is turned on. No data is used, stored or transmitted.

Cache Download Data

The App does cache downloaded data from news, event feeds, etc. No data is transmitted from the device.

Support

For comments, questions or help with the S&T app, contact mobilesupport@mst.edu
