Our Privacy Policy

At Game2Learn we acknowledge the importance of privacy and our legal obligations under current privacy legislation. Therefore, any information we collect is solely for the business purposes of Game2Learn. These include:

  • Account keeping purposes
  • Providing up-to-date product information to existing customers
  • Improving product service and delivery to our customers. This may involve contacting customers to seek feedback on their experiences when dealing with Game2Learn or using its products.

Any customer information collected by Game2Learn during product purchase or from customer correspondence will be held in strict confidence. We will not sell, share or distribute any information provided by our customers with any third party.

Exemptions

Please be aware that the exemptions to our privacy policy are:

  • If required by law, we will disclose customer information to the relevant authorities provided Game2Learn does not breach privacy legislation.
  • If Game2Learn is sold to another company, we may pass on customer information as part of this sales transaction. However, this would be provisional on the company observing the same privacy policy and only using the information for the purposes stated above.

Site security

A central aspect of the Game2Learn privacy policy is its site security. Game2Learn employs all reasonable internal and technical procedures to protect customer information from unauthorised  persons.

Privacy policy changes

While the fundamental emphasis of our privacy policy will remain unchanged, Game2Learn may modify its privacy policy to reflect operational imperatives or changes to privacy legislation.  These changes will be posted on the Game2Learn website if and when they occur.

Featured Product

Math Foundations

Math Foundations with Q&A is based on early learning principles and designed to foster independent learning skills. Math Foundations with Q&A helps children develop a sound understanding of:

• Grouping
• Shapes
• Measuring
• Telling time
• Ordering

Designed to also support the Foundation Year of the Australian and State Curricula and 21st century digital learning principles, Math Foundations with Q&A includes more than 340 interactive learning objects across the Learn, Play and Check sections.
