A cookie is a small piece of information which is sent to and stored on your device. Cookies do not damage your device. Some browsers and devices enable you to set your preferences so that you will be notified when you receive a cookie. This enables you to decide if you want to accept it or not. We may also use web beacons via the Services to collect information. Web beacons or "gifs", are electronic images that may be used in our Services or in our emails. We use Web beacons to deliver cookies, count visits and to tell if an email has been opened and acted upon.
