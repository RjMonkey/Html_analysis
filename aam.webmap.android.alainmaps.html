Al Ain Maps Privacy Policy for products and services

 

WE CARE ABOUT YOUR PRIVACY

Al Ain Maps is committed to respect your privacy and to comply with applicable data protection and privacy laws. Software on your device may access your information. Our products or services may contain links to other companies’ websites and services that have privacy policies of their own. Al Ain Maps is not responsible for the privacy practices of others and we recommend you read their privacy notices.

If you do not agree with this Policy, do not use our products and services or provide Al Ain Maps with your personal data.

What Information Do We Collect?

We do not collect any of your personal data or store it on our servers. Neither we store any of your queries which you make through our application. We also do not track or store your location with us.

We do use your microphone to record voice for speech recognition with a third party service from Nuance. We do not store the audio clip or try to store the translation from the clip or use the information for any other purpose than giving a correct search result back to you.

Please refer to Nuance privacy policy for the voice recognition services.
