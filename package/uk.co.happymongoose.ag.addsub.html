This privacy statement describes how Happy Mongoose Games collects and uses the personal information you provide. It also describes the choices available to you regarding our use of your personal information and how you can access and update this information.

Basic information

We collect the following information from you:

We use this information to improve our App and marketing efforts, but we don't pass it on to anyone else.
