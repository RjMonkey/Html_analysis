PRIVACY POLICY

Last modified: Feb 14, 2017 (view archived versions)

SATSUMA DROID APPS (“Service”, “Satsuma Drod”, "we," "us," or "our") collects information from you in order to provide better service and a better user experience. Your privacy is a top priority to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

  • What information we use and how we use it:
    1. Read phone status and identity

      This permission allows SATSUMA DROID APPS to recognize an incoming / outgoing call and to switch between the User System interface and call interface or announces / shows the caller name while some one is calling, you will identify it without looking to your smart phone.

    2. Read your text messages (SMS or MMS)

      We do not save or upload your messages. We use this permission to read your text messages when you search messages in search bar or announce the sender name while some one is send you a message, you will identify it without looking to your smart phone.

    3. Take pictures and videos

      This permission allows SATSUMA DROID APPS to use your device’s camera to take photos / videos and turn ON/OFF Camera Flash. We do not save or upload your photos/videos.

    4. Approximate location (network-based)

      This permission allows SATSUMA DROID APPS to identify and display your location on map or apps installed by anonymous surrounding users and to recommend popular apps based on users’ location. This information is untraceable.

    5. Read your contacts

      SATSUMA DROID APPS does not save or upload your contacts. Permission to access contact information is used when you search contacts in SATSUMA DROID APPS search bar.

    6. Read calendar events plus confidential information

      This information is used only in the SATSUMA DROID APPS to remind you of your latest calendar events. We do not save or upload any of your calendar information.

    7. Modify or delete the contents of your USB storage

      This permission allows SATSUMA DROID APPS to delete app data(image, text, audio etc..) stored on their SD card.

    8. Read the contents of your USB storage

      This permission allows SATSUMA DROID APPS to view app data(image, text, audio etc..) stored on their SD card.

    9. Set an alarm

      This permission allows users to access the device's alarm through SATSUMA DROID APPS clock widget.

    10. Find accounts on the devices

      SATSUMA DROID APPS does not recognize or save your account information for any programs. SATSUMA DROID APPS only detects whether users have a Google account linked with the device which helps us confirm the state of Google Service and provide users with appropriate application download and update methods.

    11. Read Google service configuration

      This information is used to acquire advertising ID. SATSUMA DROID APPS provide users with better advertising service by using such anonymous ID.

    12. Change network connectivity

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS settings and notification toolbar, in order to change/check network connectivity.

    13. Connect and disconnect from Wi-Fi

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS settings and notification toolbar in order to connect and disconnect from Wi-Fi.

    14. Full network access

      This permission is used to access the device’s network for certain functions including receiving update notifications or accessing app classification labels.

    15. View network connections

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS settings and notification toolbar, in order to view network connections.

    16. View Wi-Fi connections

      TThis permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS settings and notification toolbar, in order to view Wi-Fi connections.

    17. Access Bluetooth settings

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS settings, in order to turn on and off the Bluetooth.

    18. Pair with Bluetooth devices

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS settings, in order to pair with Bluetooth devices.

    19. Close other apps

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS Boost in order to turn off the back-end apps and make the phone run faster.

    20. Retrieve running apps

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS Boost in order to view the running apps.

    21. Run as startup

      SATSUMA DROID APPS will run as startup to provide users with a launcher service.

    22. Draw over other apps

      This permission allows the SATSUMA DROID APPS Free Swipe tab to be displayed as a floating window on other apps.

    23. Control vibration

      This permission allows SATSUMA DROID APPS to make the phone vibrate once after users set the device on vibrate, confirming the setting has been turned on.

    24. Adjust your wallpaper size

      This permission allows users to crop SATSUMA DROID APPS wallpaper to the desired size.

    25. Set wallpaper

      This permission allows users to set a selected SATSUMA DROID APPS image as their wallpaper.

    26. Read sync settings

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS power’s save mode, and allows SATSUMA DROID APPS to recognize whether sync settings are turned on or off.

    27. Toggle sync on and off

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS power’s save mode, and allows SATSUMA DROID APPS to synchronize with the user's sync settings.

    28. Expand/collapse status bar

      This permission is used for the gesture feature of SATSUMA DROID APPS User System to expand and collapse the status bar.

    29. Install shortcuts

      This permission is used to install shortcuts on other launchers, so users can continue to use SATSUMA DROID APPS related functions.

    30. Measure app storage space

      This permission is used to acquire the amount of storage space used by an application.

    31. Modify system settings

      This permission is used in SATSUMA DROID APPS settings, in order to switch or adjust ringtone, vibration and brightness level of the screen.

    32. Read Home settings and shortcuts

      This permission is used to acquire the user’s Home settings and shortcuts, in order to maintain user preferences.

    33. Uninstall shortcuts

      This permission allows SATSUMA DROID APPS to delete the shortcuts created by SATSUMA DROID APPS in other Android launchers.
